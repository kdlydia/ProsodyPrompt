SLIDE 09  (WHY this matters — octave error causes wrong prosody label)
   09_WHY_octave_error_causes_wrong_prosody_label.png

   WHAT TO SAY: "Here is why this matters for prosody labelling. The equation
   ΔF₀ = 12·log₂(offset/onset) drives the / \ - labels. If the onset is wrong
   by 12 ST (an octave), the movement calculation is off by ±12 ST — which
   flips a rising syllable to a falling label or vice versa. Left panel: what
   Praat reports → the label comes out '-' (level). Right panel: what pYIN
   sees → the same syllable correctly labelled '/' (rising)."
